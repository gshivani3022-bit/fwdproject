⚙️ Process

How FeedForward Works

From canteen kitchen to community table in 4 simple steps.

🍱

Canteen Logs Surplus

Staff log leftover food by type & quantity after each meal via app or IoT scale.

📡

System Sends Alert

Platform matches surplus to nearest ashrams/NGOs and fires real-time notifications.

🚚

Volunteer Picks Up

Registered driver or NGO vehicle collects within 2 hours of the alert.

🙏

Food Reaches Needy

Delivered to Anand Ashram, old age home or community feeding point. Impact logged.
